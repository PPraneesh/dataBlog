The Inception of Data Science

Data science and Maths

Data science and mathematics are closely related as data science relies heavily on mathematical concepts and techniques to analyze and interpret large and complex data sets. Mathematics provides the foundation for many of the statistical and machine learning algorithms used in data science, such as linear regression, clustering, and neural networks. These mathematical methods allow data scientists to extract valuable insights from data and make informed decisions based on those insights. In essence, data science is the practical application of mathematical principles to solve real-world problems related to data analysis and prediction.
